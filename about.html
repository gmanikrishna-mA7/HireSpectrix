About HireSpectrix

Smart Detection. Safer Careers.

🚀Our Mission

HireSpectrix is built to protect job seekers from rising online job scams. With thousands of fake job postings circulating daily, our mission is to provide a simple and intelligent system that helps users identify suspicious opportunities instantly.

⚠️The Problem

Many job seekers fall victim to fraudulent job offers that demand registration fees, use fake company details, or promise unrealistic salaries. These scams not only cause financial loss but also compromise personal data.

💡Our Solution

HireSpectrix analyzes job postings using multiple parameters such as salary patterns, email authenticity, website validation, and suspicious keywords. It then calculates a fraud risk score and provides clear insights to help users make safe decisions.

✨Key Features

  • ✅Fraud Risk Score (Safe / Medium / High)
  • ✅Visual Risk Meter with Percentage
  • ✅Detection of Suspicious Keywords
  • ✅Email & Website Validation
  • ✅Smart Tips to Avoid Job Scams
